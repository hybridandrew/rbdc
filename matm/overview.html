The Story So Far

In the year 1491, a group of strangers gathered at the Driftwood Tavern in Neverwinter, drawn together by fate‚Äîor perhaps by the scheming of forces beyond their understanding. The dwarf Gundren Rockseeker hired them for a simple task: escort supplies to the frontier town of Phandalin.

But nothing is simple when ancient magic stirs. Gundren claimed to have found the lost Wave Echo Cave, home to the legendary Forge of Spells. Before they could learn more, he was captured by goblins working for a mysterious figure known only as the Black Spider.

The party fought through goblin caves, stared down wolves by firelight, and waged war against the Redbrands who had terrorized Phandalin. They've made allies, lost friends, and stumbled upon darker mysteries‚Äîincluding the whispered name of Ruxithid, a psychic tyrant from beyond the world.

Now, at level 3, they stand on the precipice of something greater. The Forge of Spells awaits. The Black Spider hunts. And fire answers to those who refuse to kneel.
