The Story So Far...

In the year 1491, fate has brought together an unlikely band of adventurers. From the bustling streets of Neverwinter to the goblin-infested caves of the wilderness, and now into the dark depths beneath Trezendar Manor, your party has faced rats, boars, goblins, bugbears, and the undead themselves. The mystery of the Black Spider looms large, and whispers of an ancient evil named Ruxithid haunt your dreams. The Forge of Spells awaits discovery, and Gundren Rockseeker's fate hangs in the balance.
