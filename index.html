About
01

New Orleans raised. Always wired. For 25 years I ran BramTech Solutions out of Covington, LA — a managed services provider built on the unglamorous but essential work of keeping other people's technology alive. Cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, client relationships that lasted a decade. Then I sold it.

The exit wasn't an ending. It was permission. Permission to stop being the person who fixes everything for everyone else and start building things that spread outward — content, stories, systems that grow rather than just sustain.

Now I'm CTO at The Why Files, a YouTube and streaming brand doing something genuinely interesting at the intersection of mystery, storytelling, and digital media. I still think in systems. I still care about craft. The context just changed.

Outside of work: I kayak. I write heavy music — the kind with breakdowns and choir sections and things that shouldn't go together but do. I watch everything and have opinions about all of it. I read. I'm based in Louisiana, moving toward Nevada. Handle: hybridxer0.
